 ## Hair Care Recommendation System##

***1. Problem Statement

	Choosing the right hair care products can be confusing because different hair conditions require different ingredients. People often use products that contain ingredients unsuitable for their hair type, which can worsen dryness, frizz, oiliness, dandruff, or damage.
	To solve this problem, we need a system that provides personalized ingredient and product recommendations based on a user’s hair condition(s).

	This project provides a simple Python-based solution that suggests good ingredients, ingredients to avoid, and supportive product brands for one or multiple hair concerns.

***2. Objectives

	To guide users in selecting hair care products based on their hair condition.
	To provide a list of beneficial ingredients for specific hair issues.
	To warn users about ingredients that may be harmful or unsuitable.
	To recommend suitable hair care brands commonly associated with each condition.
	To allow users to input multiple hair concerns at once.








***3. Scope of the Project

Included
	Hair ingredient recommendations for:
	Dry hair
	Frizzy hair
	Oily hair
	Dandruff
	Damaged hair


Ingredient lists categorized into good, bad, and recommended brands.

Ability to handle multiple hair concerns at the same time.

Display of results in an organized format.

Handling of invalid conditions.


Not Included

	Real-time product search.
	Dermatological or medical diagnosis.
	Personalized routines or step-by-step care.
	Database or file storage.

Graphical user interface (GUI).

Machine learning or smart rating systems.




***4. Target Users

	Individuals who want to understand which ingredients suit their hair type.
	Beginners interested in ingredient-based hair care.
	Students learning Python dictionaries, sets, and string processing.
	Cosmetic science enthusiasts.
	Developers creating a simple decision-based recommendation system.




***5. High-Level Features

Ingredient Recommendation:
Displays all beneficial ingredients for given hair concerns.
Ingredient Warning System:
Shows ingredients that should be avoided for those conditions.
Brand Suggestions:
Provides real-world brand names associated with each hair type need.
Handles Multiple Conditions:
Users can enter several conditions like "dry, frizzy, damaged" and get combined results.
Error Handling:
Notifies users when they enter an unknown or unsupported hair condition.

Dictionary-Based Architecture:
Clean data storage for easy expansion or modification.
